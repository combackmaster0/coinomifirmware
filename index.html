Security

Built for Maximum Security

Every firmware update is engineered with security as the top priority, protecting your assets at every layer.

🔑

Private Key Isolation

Your private keys are generated and stored exclusively on your device. They are never transmitted to any server, ensuring complete sovereignty over your funds at all times.

🛡️

Real-Time Scam Detection

The new scam detection engine cross-references transactions against a continuously updated database of malicious addresses, phishing domains, and fraudulent smart contracts.

🔐

AES-256 Encryption

All sensitive wallet data is protected with military-grade AES-256 encryption. Even if your device is compromised, your funds remain inaccessible without your passphrase.

⚡

Transaction Risk Scoring

Before you confirm any transaction, our risk engine evaluates the destination address, contract code, and network conditions — flagging anything suspicious with a clear warning.
